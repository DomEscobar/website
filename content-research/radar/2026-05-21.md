### 3. Hallucination as Exploit: Evidence-Carrying Multimodal Agents

Source: arXiv cs.AI
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19192
Score: 9
Matched signals: agent, agents, benchmark, multimodal
Why unusual: It reframes multimodal hallucination as an authorization bug: a false visual claim can unlock a click, email, extraction, or transfer.

Workflow: proposed tool call → decompose into action-critical predicates → collect typed certificates from DOM/OCR/accessibility verifiers → deterministic gate grants only supported privileges.
Prompt/tool: “For each tool call, list the action-critical claims and the external evidence required; free-form model text is not evidence.”
Risk / when not to use: Needs verifier engineering; prompt-only policies remain bypassable under this threat model.
